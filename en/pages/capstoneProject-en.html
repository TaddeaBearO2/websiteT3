CAPSTONE PROJECT

Capstone Project is a project carried out by final year students as part of increasing competency in the study program. This project aims to integrate and apply the knowledge and skills acquired during the studies. Typically implemented at the final stage of the program, the Capstone Project includes research, product development, practical solutions, or application of theoretical concepts in real contexts. Involving guidance from lecturers or mentors, students then evaluate and present the results of their projects through written reports or presentations. The Capstone Project provides useful practical experience and prepares students for challenges in the professional or academic world after graduation.
