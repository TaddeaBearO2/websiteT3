TEST COMPONENTS

The test components that will later be used to produce high voltage must be chosen carefully so that they can produce a high voltage that is appropriate to the type of test that will be measured. If breakdown voltage testing is to be carried out, the high voltage generator used must have a high enough output voltage. and stable. The following are the types of high voltage testing components.

HV9111 Silicon Rectifier

HV9112 Impulse Capacitor

HV9120 Load Capacitor

HV9141 Measuring Capacitor

HV9113 Load Capacitor

HV9122 Wave Front Resistor

HV9123 Wave Tail Resistor

HV9127 Load Resistor

HV9124 Insulating Rod

HV9125 Sphere Gap

HV9132 Electronic trigger sphere

HV9138 Electrode

HV9109 Connecting Cup, Aluminium

HV9110 Floor Pedestal, Aluminium
