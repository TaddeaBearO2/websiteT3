LECTURER PROFILE


Welcome to the High Voltage Engineering Laboratory Lecturer profile page!

With full dedication to the science of high voltage engineering, we lead an innovative journey in the High Voltage Engineering Laboratory. With a strong educational background and diverse experiences, I have a vision to dig deeper into our understanding of high voltage phenomena in the world of engineering.

In-depth education and focused research help us guide students to achieve a level of excellence in their understanding of high voltage, as well as encourage them to actively participate in research and experiments in the laboratory. We believe that supporting students' professional development is key to creating future leaders in the field of High Voltage Engineering.

In the laboratory, we are committed to providing an environment that supports collaboration, creativity and discovery. Through joint research activities, we embrace new ideas and innovate in the development of high voltage technology. We prepare our students to become experts who not only master theory, but are also ready to face real world challenges.
