HIGH VOLTAGE ENGINEERING PRACTICUM

High Voltage Engineering Practicum is a laboratory activity designed to provide students with an understanding of the concepts, techniques and equipment related to high voltage in the context of electrical engineering. This practicum aims to harmonize the theoretical knowledge obtained in lectures with practical experience in the laboratory. Through this practicum, students are expected to be able to hone their practical skills, understand the real world application of several high voltage concepts, and develop a sense of responsibility for safety when working, especially on high voltage equipment.


PRACTICUM TOOLS & MATERIALS

The High Voltage Laboratory is one of the laboratories in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Hasanuddin University. This laboratory studies science related to high voltage AC, DC and Impulse which can be assembled modularly. Each generator circuit can be built using basic high voltage components, namely High Voltage Test Transformer, Control Desk, Rectifier, Impulse Capacitor, Waveforming Resistor, Load Capacitor, and several other components such as Loading Resistor, Measuring Resistor, Earthing Switch , measuring instruments, etc.
