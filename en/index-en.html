HIGH VOLTAGE ENGINEERING & ELECTRICITY INFRASTRUCTURE

High Voltage Engineering is a branch of electrical engineering which focuses on the handling, measurement and application of high voltage in electrical systems. In this context, high voltage usually refers to voltage above 1 kW (KiloWatt) even up to several MW (MegaWatt).

High Voltage Engineering has an important role in supporting the reliability and efficiency of modern electrical systems. Experts in this field contribute to the development of new technology and a deeper understanding of high voltage phenomena, which makes this field a vital part of electrical science.

High Voltage Engineering has a direct impact on the electricity infrastructure of a country or region. Good electricity infrastructure is essential to support various sectors, including industrial, commercial and household.

By integrating High Voltage Engineering into the electrical infrastructure, a system that is more reliable, efficient, and capable of supporting the development of modern electrical technology and meeting growing energy needs can be produced.
